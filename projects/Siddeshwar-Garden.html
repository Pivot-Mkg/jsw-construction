Siddeshwar Gardens

Siddeshwar Gardens Phase I marked the beginning of a landmark development on Kolshet Road, Thane, contributing significantly to the area's rapid growth.

Its key residential tower, Harmony, is a 15-storey building offering 2 BHK and 3 BHK homes across 110,000 sq.ft of development. With scenic views of the Yeoor Hills and a swift delivery timeline of under two years, Phase I stands as a strong foundation of the Siddeshwar Gardens community.
