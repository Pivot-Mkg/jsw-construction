Glacia

Glacia is a 6-storey mixed-use development located at Tembhi Naka, just 1.5 km from Thane Railway Station.

It offers efficiently planned 2 BHK residential apartments along with commercial office spaces ranging from 350 to 850 sq.ft. With fiber-optic infrastructure on every floor and modern space utilization, Glacia is designed to support both contemporary living and business needs.
